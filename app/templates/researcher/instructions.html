Instructions for the researcher

Welcome to the video annotating tool! This tool is for making research experiments giving videos for users to annotate.

At the moment we support only Vimeo videos.

Important functionalities

  • Adding and removing videos by Vimeo video id (Configure-page)
  • Adding and removing input sliders (Configure-page)
  • Changing the used database (Configure-page)
  • Modifying instructions shown for the user in the beginning of the experiment (Configure-page)
  • Seeing algorithmically retrieved insights of the collected data (Clusters & Chart -page)
  • Exploring the collected raw data (Raw -page)
  • Exporting the collected data (Raw -page)

Limitations

  • Slow loading with chart view
  • Information security: Usernames and the data is saved as plain text to the database. As long as your database is in a safe space, everything should be fine.
